Chimney Cleaning

Chimney Sweep, Wood Burning Stove Cleaning, Gas Fireplace Cleaning, Rain Cap Installation, Tuck Pointing, Damper Issues, Crown Issues, All Chimney Services in Sacramento and 80 Miles Radius

The National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) states that chimneys, fireplaces and related vents should be inspected and cleaned at a minimum on a yearly basis to ensure the safety of your household. Burning wood inside your home, especially for several months during the year, will leave thick layers of soot and creosote in your fireplace and chimney. These substances are highly flammable by-products of burnt wood, and damaging to the lungs if breathed in. Different types of wood may cause a faster or thicker buildup of creosote. It blocks the air flow of the chimney and may cause smoke and soot to enter the house leading to possible suffocation or a household fire.
